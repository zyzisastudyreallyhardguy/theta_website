Our Vision

Our mission is to develop novel therapies ready for human testing within 5 years, while establishing ourselves as one of the top 10 research teams in AI for drug discovery within the next 5 years.

The symbol Θ looks like a loop, which represents the beauty of iterative engineering. We stand at this perspective, iteratively solving the most difficult biomedical challenges in the world.

Collaborative Team

THETA TEAM is a unique collaboration between the LTI lab in Monash School of Medicine and the TrustAGI lab in Griffith University. We are based at the Paula Fox Melanoma and Cancer Center, the largest clinical trial site in Australia.

Our team encompasses a diverse range of experts including AI scientists, structural biologists, immunologists, and medical oncologists, bringing together strong and varied backgrounds to tackle complex challenges.

Unique Expertise

What sets us apart is our combination of a strong AI foundation, world-class wet lab facilities (including cryo-ET and cryo-EM technologies), and access to patient data. This powerful combination is extremely rare worldwide and positions us to make unique contributions to the field.

Our Mission

We have two primary missions that drive our work:

  • To develop therapies ready for human clinical trials within 5 years
  • To establish ourselves as one of the top 10 teams globally in AI for drug discovery within the next 5 years

Through our interdisciplinary approach and cutting-edge technologies, we aim to accelerate the development of novel treatments that can make a real difference in patients' lives.
